Transforming Genomic Workflows

GeneXplor equips researchers, geneticists, and bioinformaticians with specialized tools to accelerate variant interpretation and structural biology.

Clinical Variant Interpretation

Streamline the ACMG classification process by unifying critical data streams into a single dashboard.

Instant Population Frequencies

Quickly determine if a rare missense variant is actually a common polymorphism in specific gnomAD geographic cohorts.

Automated Conflict Detection

Identify conflicting assertions immediately. GeneXplor flags variants labeled "Pathogenic" in ClinVar that exceed allele frequency thresholds.

One-Click Clinical Reports

Generate highly structured, PDF-ready summaries of genetic findings, curated specifically for patient reports or tumor boards.

Structural Protein Biology

Bridge the gap between genomic mutations and physical protein structures.

Functional Impact Mapping

View EXACTLY where a variant lands on an AlphaFold 3D model to see if it disrupts a crucial binding domain or catalytic site.

Domain Architecture

Visualize the linear peptide sequence alongside structural motifs (e.g., zinc fingers, kinase domains) sourced directly from UniProt.

Molecular Interactions

Explore D3-powered networks of protein-protein interactions to understand how a single gene knockout might ripple through a cellular pathway.

Biomedical Literature Tracking

Cut through the noise of thousands of publications to find exactly what matters for your gene of interest.

Research Momentum

Track the "Research Pulse" of any gene to see historical publication trends overlaid with key discovery milestones.

Curated PubMed Feeds

Access the latest studies, instantly filtered by relevance to specific diseases, variants, or biochemical pathways.

AI-Summarized Evidence

Leverage language models to condense decades of research into a plain-english "Gene Story" that explains the gene's primary function in human health.
