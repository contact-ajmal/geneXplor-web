Core Features

A comprehensive suite of tools for deep genomic analysis.

Smart Gene Search

Real-time autocomplete powered by Meilisearch. Search instantly across gene symbols, aliases, Ensembl IDs, associated diseases, variants, and chromosomal locations with Cmd/Ctrl+K support.

Gene Dashboard

A unified 12-tab analysis dashboard aggregating data from 8 distinct databases in real-time. Navigate seamlessly between variants, proteins, pathways, and publications.

Variant Explorer

Advanced data table capable of filtering, sorting, and analyzing thousands of variants. Filter by clinical significance, consequence, and allele frequency with immediate visual feedback.

3D Protein Structure

Interactive AlphaFold integration using PDBe Molstar. Visualize protein models with pLDDT confidence coloring, map variants directly onto residues, and toggle display modes.

Population Genetics

Global map visualizations of variant allele frequencies. Break down occurrence rates across 9 distinct geographic populations relying on gnomAD exome and genome datasets.

Reconciliation Engine

Cross-references assertions from ClinVar with population data from gnomAD to flag conflicts — automatically detecting variants classified as Pathogenic but common in healthy populations.

Clinical Reports

Automated generation of ACMG-formatted clinical summaries. Select specific variants and findings, and export highly-structured Markdown, PDF, or JSON reports.

Impact Simulator

An animated visual cascade showing the complete Central Dogma translation pipeline (DNA → RNA → Protein → Structure → Function) for any selected genomic variant.

Research Publications

Live feed of PubMed biomedical literature targeting the specific gene. Includes a Research Pulse timeline tracking relative publication momentum over decades.
